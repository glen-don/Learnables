The purchase screen didn't respond?
If you experience a delay after clicking "Continue," it is often due to a temporary network timeout. Please ensure you have a stable connection. If a purchase was successful but didn't show in the app, tap "Restore Purchases" in the app settings to sync your status instantly.
How can I request a refund?
As all transactions are handled by Apple, you can request a refund directly through their support portal: reportaproblem.apple.com.

üîíAccount Safety & Deletion

How do I delete my account?
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly within the app: Settings > Account > Delete Account. This action is irreversible and will remove all reading progress.
